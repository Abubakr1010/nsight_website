Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to nsights.ink. We are committed to protecting your business data and privacy. This policy explains how our application interacts with your Shopify store data to provide you with actionable insights.

2. Data We Fetch

When you connect your Shopify store(s) to nsights.ink via our secure OAuth process, we access specific data points required to generate your dashboard, including:

  • Order Information: Transaction totals, order counts, and fulfillment status.
  • Sales Data: Gross and net sales figures to calculate growth trends.
  • Store Details: Shop name, domain, and currency settings.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following "Internal Tool" functions:

  • Aggregation: Combining data from multiple stores into a single view.
  • Insights Generation: Calculating performance metrics that Shopify might not show in a combined view.
  • Monitoring: Providing real-time updates on your store's health directly to your Android device.

Note: We do not sell your store data to third parties. Data is used solely to provide the services you have requested within the app.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect the tokens that grant access to your Shopify data. Access is strictly controlled and limited to the technical necessity of providing your store insights.
