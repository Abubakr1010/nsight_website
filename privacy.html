Privacy Policy

Data Fetching & Internal Use

We fetch specific data from your connected Shopify stores to provide you with combined performance metrics. This includes:

  • Order Details: Totals, dates, and order counts.
  • Sales Performance: Revenue and growth statistics.

This data is used exclusively within our Internal Tool to provide you with insights. We do not sell or share your store data with third parties.

Security

We use encrypted OAuth tokens to access your Shopify data. Your store credentials are never stored on our servers.
